Roadmap to US Pharmacy Licensure

Step 1 – Credential Evaluation

Submit academic transcripts to NABP for evaluation. Timeline: 1–2 months.

Step 2 – TOEFL iBT

English proficiency test (minimum required scores per NABP). Connectek offers pharmacy-focused TOEFL training.

Step 3 – FPGEE

Foreign Pharmacy Graduate Equivalency Examination covering pharmaceutical sciences and US healthcare laws. Timeline: 3–6 months prep.

Step 4 – Internship Hours

Complete 1,500 hours under US-licensed pharmacist supervision.

Step 5 – NAPLEX

Clinical exam for pharmacist licensure.

Step 6 – MPJE

US law exam (varies by state).
