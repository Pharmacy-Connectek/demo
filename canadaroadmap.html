Roadmap to Canada Pharmacy Licensure

Step 1 – Document Evaluation

Submit credentials to PEBC. Timeline: 1–2 months.

Step 2 – PEBC Evaluation Exam

Covers basic pharmaceutical sciences. Timeline: 4–6 months prep.

Step 3 – English Proficiency (TOEFL/OET)

Required for non-native speakers.

Step 4 – PEBC Qualifying Exam (Part I & II)

Part I: MCQ knowledge test. Part II: OSCE practical skills exam.

Step 5 – Jurisprudence Exam

Provincial law exam.

Step 6 – Internship/Residency

Practical training in Canadian pharmacies.
