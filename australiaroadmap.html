Roadmap to Australia Pharmacy Licensure

Step 1 – Eligibility Check

Verify degree recognition with the Australian Pharmacy Council (APC).

Step 2 – KAPS Exam

Knowledge Assessment of Pharmaceutical Sciences. Timeline: 4–6 months prep.

Step 3 – English Proficiency (OET/IELTS)

Meet minimum language requirements.

Step 4 – APC Skills Assessment

Complete formal skills assessment with APC.

Step 5 – Internship

Supervised practice period in Australia.

Step 6 – Pharmacy Board Registration

Final licensing step to work as a pharmacist in Australia.
